**3) Near-Term Catalysts (0–12 months)**

**1. Sustained Double-Digit Subscription Revenue Growth**

*   **Catalyst Title:** Sustained Double-Digit Subscription Revenue Growth
*   **Type:** Financial
*   **Description (1–2 sentences):** Karooooo's primary revenue driver, Cartrack's subscription revenue, has been consistently growing at a high rate, reaching 19% year-over-year growth in Q1 2026. Continued execution in customer acquisition and upselling could maintain or accelerate this growth.
*   **Mechanism:** Higher subscription revenue directly translates to higher gross and net income, assuming stable margins. Sustained high growth will likely lead to a re-rating of the stock's valuation multiple by the market, as it demonstrates the durability of the business model.
*   **Timing:** Ongoing, with the next key data point at the Q2 2026 earnings release (expected October 2025).
*   **Quantification:**
    *   **Low:** 15% subscription revenue growth for FY2026.
    *   **Base:** 18% subscription revenue growth for FY2026. This would result in approximately ZAR 5,145 million in subscription revenue for the full year (based on Q1 2026 of ZAR 1,138 million annualised and grown).
    *   **High:** 22% subscription revenue growth for FY2026.
*   **Estimated Price Impact:** Large (>30%)
*   **Probability / Confidence (0–100%):** 85% - The company has a strong track record of subscriber growth, and management has expressed confidence in continued healthy organic growth.
*   **Key Dependencies / Conditions:** Continued strong net subscriber additions and maintaining low churn.
*   **Monitoring Triggers / KPIs:**
    *   Net subscriber additions per quarter (Q1 2026: 84,013).
    *   Subscription revenue growth rate (Q1 2026: 19%).
    *   Annual Recurring Revenue (ARR) (Q1 2026: ZAR 4,574 million).
*   **Anti-Catalysts / Risks:**
    *   Increased competition in key markets could slow subscriber growth.
    *   Macroeconomic headwinds in South Africa or Southeast Asia could reduce demand.
*   **Suggested Investor Action:** Buy - The current valuation may not fully reflect the potential for sustained high growth.
