### **3) Near-Term Catalysts (0–12 months)**

**1. Catalyst Title: 2025 Restructuring Plan**
*   **Type:** Operational
*   **Description (1–2 sentences):** On May 15, 2024, the Board of Directors approved a restructuring plan to rebalance the company's cost base, aiming to improve profitability and cash flow. The plan involves an estimated $70 million to $90 million in pre-tax charges in Fiscal 2025.
*   **Mechanism:** The restructuring plan is expected to reduce operating expenses and improve margins, leading to higher net income and free cash flow. This would increase the company's valuation and attract investors.
*   **Timing:** The majority of the restructuring charges are expected to be incurred in Fiscal 2025 (ending March 31, 2026).
*   **Quantification:**
    *   **Low Case:** $70 million in restructuring charges, leading to a 5% improvement in operating margin.
    *   **Base Case:** $80 million in restructuring charges, leading to a 7.5% improvement in operating margin.
    *   **High Case:** $90 million in restructuring charges, leading to a 10% improvement in operating margin.
*   **Estimated Price Impact:** Medium (15-25%) - A significant improvement in profitability could lead to a re-rating of the stock.
*   **Probability / Confidence (0–100%):** 90% - The plan has been approved by the board and is already being implemented.
*   **Key Dependencies / Conditions:** Successful execution of the restructuring plan without disrupting key business operations.
*   **Monitoring Triggers / KPIs:**
    *   Quarterly reports on restructuring charges and progress.
    *   Improvements in operating margin and SG&A expenses as a percentage of revenue.
    *   Management commentary on the plan's progress in earnings calls.
*   **Anti-Catalysts / Risks:**
    *   The restructuring could negatively impact employee morale and productivity.
    *   The expected cost savings may not fully materialize.
    *   The plan could be a sign of deeper underlying issues with the business.
*   **Suggested Investor Action:** Watch - Monitor the execution of the restructuring plan and its impact on the company's financial performance.
