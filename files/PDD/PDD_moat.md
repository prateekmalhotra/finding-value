**1) Network Effects**

*   **Reasoning:** PDD's large and growing user base creates a powerful network effect. More buyers attract more sellers, who in turn offer a wider variety of goods, which attracts more buyers. The company's social commerce model, with its emphasis on group buying, further strengthens these network effects.
*   **Evidence:**
    *   PDD continues to grow its user base, a key driver of its network effects. (implied from revenue growth in Q1 2025 Earnings Call Transcript, May 27, 2025)
    *   The company's business model is centered around a "group-buying engine" which inherently relies on network effects. (Stock Titan search result snippet)
*   **Score:** 4 / 5

**2) Switching Costs**

*   **Reasoning:** Switching costs for both buyers and sellers are relatively low. Buyers can easily switch to other e-commerce platforms, and sellers can and do multi-home on several platforms.
