**1) Products/Services with Long Runway**
**Question:** Does the company have products or services with sufficient market potential to make possible a sizable increase in sales for at least several years?
**Reasoning & Evidence:** Flowers Foods operates in the large but mature U.S. packaged bakery market. While core categories like traditional bread face volume softness, the company is strategically targeting growth segments. Per its latest 10-Q, sales of premium, better-for-you products (organic, keto, gluten-free) are increasing. The recent acquisition of Simple Mills (February 21, 2025) and the expansion of its Dave's Killer Bread brand into snacking are key initiatives to capture growth in adjacent categories and align with consumer trends. The company's long-term goal is 1% to 2% annual sales growth before future acquisitions, indicating a strategy of steady gains rather than rapid expansion.
