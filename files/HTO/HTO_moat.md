### **Top 3 Moat Erosion Scenarios**
1.  **Adverse Regulatory Changes:** State utility commissions could impose unfavorable rate structures, limit capital expenditure recovery, or (in an extreme scenario) municipalize the service area, eroding the primary regulatory moat. (AInvest, July 29, 2025).
2.  **Technological Disruption:** While unlikely in the next 15 years, a breakthrough in point-of-use water purification and reclamation at a residential scale could theoretically reduce reliance on centralized networks, eroding switching costs and scale advantages.
3.  **Operational Mismanagement:** A major failure in infrastructure leading to significant service disruptions or contamination events could result in severe regulatory penalties and loss of public trust, potentially jeopardizing the company's operating licenses.
