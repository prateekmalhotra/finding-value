*   **Anti-Catalysts / Risks:** Delays in Network 2.0 implementation, higher-than-expected integration costs, or unionization efforts could derail cost-saving efforts.

*   **Probability / Confidence (0–100%):** 85%. The company has publicly announced its intention to pursue the spin-off.
*   **Key Dependencies / Conditions:** Favorable market conditions, regulatory approvals, and a successful execution of the spin-off plan.
*   **Monitoring Triggers / KPIs:** Announcements on the progress of the spin-off, including the filing of a Form 10 registration statement with the SEC.
*   **Anti-Catalysts / Risks:** The spin-off could be delayed or canceled due to unfavorable market conditions or regulatory hurdles. The separation could also result in dis-synergies that negatively impact the performance of both companies.
