### **Moat Duration Justification**
The durability of ADP's moat is estimated to be 10–15 years. This is primarily underpinned by extremely high switching costs and a powerful, trusted brand, which are slow to erode. While technology cycles will change, the embedded nature of ADP's services in client operations and the immense challenge of replicating its global compliance and data infrastructure provide a long runway of durable competitive advantage.

### **Top 3 Moat Erosion Scenarios**
1.  **Disruptive Technology:** A competitor could develop a radically simpler, AI-native HCM platform that significantly reduces implementation friction and data migration costs, thereby lowering switching costs over time. (Inference based on technology risks cited in Form 10-K, June 30, 2024)
2.  **Major Data Breach:** A significant cybersecurity incident that compromises sensitive client data could severely damage ADP's brand reputation for trust and security, causing an exodus of clients despite high switching costs. (Risk Factor highlighted in Form 10-K, June 30, 2024)
3.  **Intensified Competition from ERP providers:** Enterprise Resource Planning (ERP) providers could more aggressively bundle HCM modules with their core financial software, leveraging their existing client relationships to displace ADP's solutions. (Competition risk cited in Form 10-K, June 30, 2024)

### **Short Verdict Paragraph**
