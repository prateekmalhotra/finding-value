### **3) Near-Term Catalysts (0–12 months)**

**1. Catalyst Title: Share Repurchase Program Execution**
*   **Type:** Corporate Action
*   **Description (1–2 sentences):** Lowe's has a significant share repurchase program authorized by its Board of Directors. As of January 31, 2025, there was $10.8 billion remaining under this program.
*   **Mechanism:** Share repurchases reduce the number of outstanding shares, which increases earnings per share (EPS). Higher EPS, assuming a constant price-to-earnings (P/E) ratio, leads to a higher stock price.
*   **Timing:** Ongoing, with potential for accelerated repurchases in the next 0-12 months.
*   **Quantification:**
    *   **Low Scenario:** Repurchase of $4 billion in shares. At an average price of $230/share, this would retire approximately 17.4 million shares.
    *   **Base Scenario:** Repurchase of $6 billion in shares, retiring approximately 26.1 million shares.
    *   **High Scenario:** Repurchase of $8 billion in shares, retiring approximately 34.8 million shares.
*   **Estimated Price Impact:** Small (2-5%). The impact is dependent on the pace of repurchases and the prevailing stock price.
*   **Probability / Confidence (0–100%):** 90%. The company has a consistent history of share repurchases.
*   **Key Dependencies / Conditions:** Continued strong cash flow generation and management's commitment to the repurchase program.
*   **Monitoring Triggers / KPIs:** Quarterly reports of share repurchase activity in Form 10-Q and 10-K filings.
*   **Anti-Catalysts / Risks:** A significant downturn in business could lead to a reduction in the pace of repurchases. Management may choose to allocate capital to other initiatives, such as acquisitions.
*   **Suggested Investor Action:** Watch. Monitor the pace of share repurchases in quarterly filings.

**2. Catalyst Title: Pro Customer Penetration Growth**
*   **Type:** Operational
*   **Description (1–2 sentences):** A key pillar of Lowe's "Total Home" strategy is to increase penetration with professional (Pro) customers. This involves enhancing product assortment, improving job site delivery, and a redesigned loyalty program.
*   **Mechanism:** Pro customers represent a larger and more consistent revenue stream than DIY customers. Increasing the mix of Pro customers can lead to higher average ticket sizes, greater sales volume, and improved margins.
*   **Timing:** Ongoing, with results expected to be visible in the next 0-12 months.
*   **Quantification:**
    *   **Low Scenario:** 2% increase in Pro sales.
    *   **Base Scenario:** 5% increase in Pro sales.
    *   **High Scenario:** 10% increase in Pro sales.
*   **Estimated Price Impact:** Medium (5-15%).
*   **Probability / Confidence (0–100%):** 75%. Management has consistently emphasized the importance of the Pro customer.
*   **Key Dependencies / Conditions:** Successful execution of the Pro-focused initiatives, and a healthy construction and remodeling market.
*   **Monitoring Triggers / KPIs:** Commentary on Pro sales growth in quarterly earnings calls and reports. Market share data for the Pro segment.
*   **Anti-Catalysts / Risks:** Increased competition for Pro customers from other retailers and distributors. A slowdown in the construction and remodeling market.
*   **Suggested Investor Action:** Watch. Monitor earnings calls for updates on the Pro strategy.

**3. Catalyst Title: DIY Discretionary Spending Rebound**
*   **Type:** Macro
*   **Description (1–2 sentences):** A significant portion of Lowe's sales comes from DIY customers. A rebound in discretionary spending, driven by factors such as lower inflation and interest rates, could lead to a significant increase in sales.
*   **Mechanism:** Lower inflation and interest rates increase consumers' disposable income and their willingness to spend on home improvement projects. This would lead to higher sales and earnings for Lowe's.
*   **Timing:** 6-12 months, dependent on macroeconomic trends.
*   **Quantification:**
    *   **Low Scenario:** 1% increase in DIY sales.
    *   **Base Scenario:** 3% increase in DIY sales.
    *   **High Scenario:** 5% increase in DIY sales.
*   **Estimated Price Impact:** Medium (10-20%).
*   **Probability / Confidence (0–100%):** 60%. This catalyst is highly dependent on external macroeconomic factors.
*   **Key Dependencies / Conditions:** A sustained decrease in inflation and interest rates.
*   **Monitoring Triggers / KPIs:** Consumer confidence indices, inflation data, and Federal Reserve interest rate decisions.
*   **Anti-Catalysts / Risks:** A prolonged period of high inflation and interest rates. A recession leading to a decrease in consumer spending.
*   **Suggested Investor Action:** Watch. Monitor key macroeconomic indicators.
