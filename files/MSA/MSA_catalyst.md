*   **Timing:** Ongoing, with potential for acceleration in the next 0–12 months, depending on cash flow and market conditions.
*   **Quantification:**
    *   **Low Scenario:** Repurchase of $25 million of stock.
    *   **Base Scenario:** Repurchase of $50 million of stock.
    *   **High Scenario:** Repurchase of $75 million of stock.

*   **Probability / Confidence (0–100%):** 75%. The company has been actively using the program, though modestly. The probability of continued repurchases is high, but the magnitude is uncertain.
*   **Key Dependencies / Conditions:** Sufficient free cash flow generation and management's view on the stock's valuation.
*   **Monitoring Triggers / KPIs:**
    *   Quarterly "Use of Proceeds" disclosure in 10-Q/10-K filings.
    *   Free cash flow generation.
    *   Management commentary on capital allocation in earnings calls.
*   **Anti-Catalysts / Risks:**

    *   A significant downturn in business could reduce cash flow available for repurchases.
    *   Management may choose to prioritize debt repayment over share repurchases.
*   **Suggested Investor Action:** Watch. Monitor the pace of buybacks in quarterly filings.
