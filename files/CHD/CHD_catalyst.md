### 3. **Near-Term Catalysts (0–12 months)**

**1. Catalyst Title:** Strategic Divestiture of Underperforming Brands
   - **Type:** Corporate Action
   - **Description (1–2 sentences):** On May 1, 2025, the company announced its intention to shut down or sell the Flawless, Spinbrush, and Waterpik showerhead businesses, which collectively generate approximately $150.0 million in annual net sales. The company expects to record a charge of $60.0 to $80.0 million in the second quarter of 2025 related to these actions.
   - **Mechanism:** The divestiture of these lower-margin, underperforming businesses will allow management to focus on its core "power brands" and reallocate resources to higher-growth opportunities. This will improve the company's overall margin profile and could lead to a higher valuation multiple.
   - **Timing:** The announcement was made on May 1, 2025, with the associated charges expected in the second quarter of 2025. The full impact will likely be realized over the next 6-12 months.
   - **Quantification:**
      - *Low Scenario:* The company successfully divests the businesses but at the lower end of the expected valuation, with minimal impact on margins.
      - *Base Scenario:* The company divests the businesses, and the reallocation of resources leads to a 50-basis-point improvement in operating margin.
      - *High Scenario:* The company divests the businesses at a favorable valuation and the margin improvement exceeds 75 basis points.
   - **Estimated Price Impact:** Small (5-10%)
   - **Probability / Confidence (0–100%):** 90%. The company has publicly announced its intentions, making this a high-probability event.
   - **Key Dependencies / Conditions:** Successful execution of the divestitures at reasonable valuations.
   - **Monitoring Triggers / KPIs:** Announcements of the sale of the businesses, financial impact in the Q2 2025 10-Q, and management commentary in subsequent earnings calls.
   - **Anti-Catalysts / Risks:** Inability to find buyers for the businesses, or a lower-than-expected sale price.
   - **Suggested Investor Action:** Watch.
