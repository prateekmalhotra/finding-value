### **3) Near-Term Catalysts (0–12 months)**

**1. Catalyst Title:** Share Repurchase Program Execution
*   **Type:** Corporate Action
*   **Description (1–2 sentences):** Chewy has an active share repurchase program with $406.7 million remaining for future repurchases as of February 2, 2025. The company has been actively repurchasing shares, including a $50.0 million repurchase in the fourth quarter of fiscal year 2024.
*   **Mechanism:** Aggressive share repurchases will reduce the number of outstanding shares, which in turn increases earnings per share (EPS). Higher EPS can lead to a higher stock price, assuming the price-to-earnings (P/E) multiple remains constant. It also signals management's confidence in the company's future prospects, which can attract new investors.
*   **Timing:** August 2025 – August 2026. The program has no expiration date, but given recent activity, it is likely to be utilized in the near term.
*   **Quantification:**
    *   **Low Scenario:** The company repurchases an additional $100 million of stock.
    *   **Base Scenario:** The company repurchases an additional $250 million of stock.
    *   **High Scenario:** The company fully utilizes the remaining $406.7 million of the repurchase program.
*   **Estimated Price Impact:** Medium (+10-20%). A significant reduction in share count can lead to a meaningful increase in EPS, which is a key driver of stock valuation.
*   **Probability / Confidence (0–100%):** 85%. The company has been actively repurchasing shares, which suggests a high probability of continued activity.
*   **Key Dependencies / Conditions:** The company's stock price must be at a level that management deems attractive for repurchases. The company must also have sufficient cash flow to fund the repurchases without impacting its growth initiatives.
*   **Monitoring Triggers / KPIs:**
    *   Quarterly reports on share repurchase activity in Form 10-Q and 10-K filings.
    *   Company announcements of any new or expanded repurchase programs.
    *   Free cash flow generation.
*   **Anti-Catalysts / Risks:**
    *   A significant increase in the stock price could make repurchases less attractive.
    *   A deterioration in business fundamentals could lead the company to preserve cash instead of repurchasing shares.
    *   Insider selling could offset the positive impact of share repurchases.
*   **Suggested Investor Action:** Watch. Monitor the company's quarterly reports for updates on the share repurchase program.

*   **Quantification:**

    *   **Base Scenario:** The company opens an additional 15-20 clinics in the next 12 months.
    *   **High Scenario:** The company opens more than 25 new clinics and demonstrates strong early performance in terms of customer acquisition and revenue per clinic.
*   **Estimated Price Impact:** Medium (+15-25%). Successful execution of this strategy could lead to a significant re-rating of the stock as investors recognize the long-term growth potential of the pet healthcare business.
*   **Probability / Confidence (0–100%):** 90%. The company has already opened eight clinics and has indicated that this is a key growth initiative.
*   **Key Dependencies / Conditions:** The company must be able to successfully manage the operational complexities of running veterinary clinics. It must also be able to attract and retain qualified veterinarians and other staff.
*   **Monitoring Triggers / KPIs:**
    *   Company announcements of new clinic openings.
    *   Quarterly updates on the performance of the Chewy Vet Care segment, if disclosed.
    *   Customer reviews and feedback on the new clinics.
*   **Anti-Catalysts / Risks:**

    *   The company could face challenges in attracting and retaining qualified veterinarians.
