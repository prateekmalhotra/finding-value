### **3. Near-Term Catalysts (0–12 months)**

**1. Catalyst Title:** Successful Execution of 2026 Restructuring Plan
*   **Type:** Operational
*   **Description (1–2 sentences):** Autodesk initiated a restructuring plan in the first quarter of fiscal 2026 to optimize its go-to-market organization and reallocate resources to strategic priorities such as cloud, platform, and artificial intelligence. The plan is expected to involve a workforce reduction and other cost-saving measures.
*   **Mechanism:** The restructuring could lead to improved operating margins and profitability, which would in turn increase earnings per share and potentially lead to a higher stock price. By reallocating resources to high-growth areas, the company may also accelerate revenue growth.
*   **Timing:** The plan was initiated in the first quarter of fiscal 2026, and its effects are expected to be realized over the next 12 months.
*   **Quantification:**
    *   **Low Scenario:** Minimal cost savings and disruption from the restructuring, leading to a 1% improvement in operating margin.
    *   **Base Scenario:** A 2-3% improvement in operating margin, resulting in an additional $120-$180 million in operating income (based on FY2025 revenue of $6.13 billion).
    *   **High Scenario:** A 4% or greater improvement in operating margin, adding over $240 million to operating income.
*   **Estimated Price Impact:** Medium (10-20%)
*   **Probability / Confidence (0–100%):** 75%. The plan is already in motion, but the full impact and potential for disruption are not yet known.
*   **Key Dependencies / Conditions:** Successful execution of the plan without significant disruption to sales or employee morale.
*   **Monitoring Triggers / KPIs:** Quarterly earnings reports for updates on restructuring charges, operating margin, and employee-related costs.
*   **Anti-Catalysts / Risks:**
    *   The restructuring could negatively impact employee morale and productivity.
    *   Cost savings may not be as significant as anticipated.
    *   The reallocation of resources may not lead to the expected growth in strategic priority areas.
*   **Suggested Investor Action:** Watch. Monitor the company's progress on the restructuring plan in the upcoming quarterly reports.

*   **Monitoring Triggers / KPIs:** Quarterly earnings reports for the amount of shares repurchased and the remaining authorization.
