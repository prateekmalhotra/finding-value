    *   **High Scenario:** The company repurchases the full $21.0 million at an average price of $45/share, retiring ~467,000 shares.

*   **Probability / Confidence (0–100%):** 85%. The company has a history of executing on its buyback program.
*   **Key Dependencies / Conditions:** The company must have sufficient free cash flow to execute the buyback without taking on excessive debt.
*   **Monitoring Triggers / KPIs:** Quarterly reports on share repurchase activity in Form 10-Q and 10-K filings.
*   **Anti-Catalysts / Risks:** A significant downturn in the business could reduce free cash flow and halt the buyback program.
