*   **Timing:** August 2025 – August 2026
*   **Quantification:**
    *   Low: 5% DTC comparable sales growth
    *   Base: 10% DTC comparable sales growth
    *   High: 15% DTC comparable sales growth
*   **Estimated Price Impact:** 10-20%
*   **Probability / Confidence (0–100%):** 70%. The company has shown strong recent performance, but sustained growth is not guaranteed.
*   **Key Dependencies / Conditions:** Continued brand heat, successful new product launches, and a stable macroeconomic environment.
*   **Monitoring Triggers / KPIs:** Quarterly reports on DTC comparable sales growth, traffic to the company's website and stores, and social media engagement.
*   **Anti-Catalysts / Risks:** A slowdown in consumer spending on luxury goods, increased competition, or a failure to innovate.
