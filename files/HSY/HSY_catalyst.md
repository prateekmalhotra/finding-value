### **8) Net Catalysts Balance & Short-Term Market Signal**

The near-term catalysts for Hershey appear to be slightly positive. The "Advancing Agility & Automation Initiative" has the potential to drive meaningful margin improvement and EPS growth, while the ongoing share repurchase program provides a steady tailwind. However, these positives are tempered by the significant risk of cocoa price volatility, which could pressure gross margins and offset the benefits of the cost-saving initiatives. The recent insider selling, particularly by top executives, also warrants caution. Overall, the short-term market signal is neutral to slightly positive, with the stock's performance likely to be heavily influenced by the direction of cocoa prices and the company's ability to execute on its cost-saving plans.
