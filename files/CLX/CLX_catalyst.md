### **3) Near-Term Catalysts (0–12 months)**

**1. Catalyst Title:** Margin Recovery to Pre-Pandemic Levels
*   **Type:** Financial
*   **Description (1–2 sentences):** The company is on a clear trajectory to restore gross margins to pre-pandemic levels, driven by pricing actions, cost savings initiatives, and the shedding of lower-margin businesses. Management has stated they are on track to achieve this in fiscal year 2025.
*   **Mechanism:** Higher gross margins directly translate to improved profitability and higher net income. This increases earnings per share (EPS), and with a stable P/E multiple, leads to a higher stock price.
*   **Timing:** Fiscal year 2025 (July 1, 2024 - June 30, 2025).
*   **Quantification:**
    *   Low Scenario: Gross margin expands by 150 basis points.
    *   Base Scenario: Gross margin expands by 250 basis points.
    *   High Scenario: Gross margin expands by 350 basis points.
*   **Estimated Price Impact:** 10-20%
*   **Probability / Confidence (0–100%):** 85%. Rationale: The company has already delivered seven consecutive quarters of margin expansion.
*   **Key Dependencies / Conditions:** Stable commodity costs, successful execution of cost-saving programs.
*   **Monitoring Triggers / KPIs:** Quarterly gross margin figures in earnings reports.
*   **Anti-Catalysts / Risks:** Renewed commodity inflation, increased competitive promotional activity, supply chain disruptions.
*   **Suggested Investor Action:** Watch.

*   **Mechanism:** Share repurchases reduce the number of outstanding shares, which increases EPS. This can lead to a higher stock price, assuming the P/E multiple remains constant. It also signals management's confidence in the company's future prospects.
*   **Timing:** Ongoing, with potential for acceleration in fiscal year 2025.
*   **Quantification:**
    *   Low Scenario: $250 million in share repurchases.
    *   Base Scenario: $500 million in share repurchases.
    *   High Scenario: $750 million in share repurchases.
*   **Estimated Price Impact:** 5-10%
*   **Probability / Confidence (0–100%):** 75%. Rationale: The company has a history of returning cash to shareholders, though the pace of repurchases can be variable.
*   **Key Dependencies / Conditions:** Free cash flow generation, alternative uses of cash (e.g., acquisitions).
*   **Monitoring Triggers / KPIs:** Quarterly reports on share repurchase activity in SEC filings.
*   **Anti-Catalysts / Risks:** A large acquisition could divert cash from repurchases. A significant downturn in profitability could also lead to the suspension of the program.
*   **Suggested Investor Action:** Buy.
