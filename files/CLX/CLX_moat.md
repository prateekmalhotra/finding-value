2.  **Major Retailer Shift:** A strategic shift by a key customer like Walmart (representing over 25% of sales) to prioritize their own private labels or drastically reduce shelf space for Clorox products could significantly impair the company's distribution advantage. (Form 10-K, Aug 8, 2024)
