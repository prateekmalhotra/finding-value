3.  **Methodology for Terminal Value:** The original model uses the Gordon Growth Model (GGM) as the primary method and the Exit Multiple as a cross-check. For cyclical industrial companies, it is often more practical and grounded in reality to use an Exit Multiple based on industry comparables as the primary method and use GGM to check for the implied long-term growth rate.
