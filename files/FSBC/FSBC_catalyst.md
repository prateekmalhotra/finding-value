    *   **Low:** 5% annual loan growth.

    *   **High:** 15% annual loan growth.

*   **Key Dependencies / Conditions:** Favorable economic conditions in Northern California, continued success of the San Francisco Bay Area expansion, and the ability to maintain credit quality.
*   **Monitoring Triggers / KPIs:**
    *   Quarterly loan and deposit growth rates (reported in 10-Q and 10-K filings).
